About Me

My name is Ryan Dean. I was originally born in Hollywood, Florida but moved to Toms River, New Jersey when I was 3 years old. I lived there until I was 9 years old and then moved to Jackson, New Jersey where I lived until I came to college. Now I live in Columbia, SC. Some hobbies I have include working out, drawing, watching movies and tv shows, working, and hanging out with my friends....

Right now I spend my summers at home where I lifeguard on Long Beach Island, New Jersey. I have spent my summers there for as long as I can remember and I attribute that to my love for the beach. Next summer I am planning on staying in Columbia and looking for an internship here.
